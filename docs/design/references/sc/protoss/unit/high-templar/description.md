# High Templar

Reference image: `liquipedia-high-templar.jpg`

The reference is a robed caster with bright psi energy. It should be a small robed silhouette with a larger energy read than body read.

Distinctive features:

- Small central hood/robe.
- Raised shoulder/arm suggestions.
- Large psi aura/halo.
- No weapon.

SVG plan:

- `panels`: small robe/hood teardrop.
- `lines`: two soft side arcs and one halo arc.
- `cores`: bright head/chest psi core.
- Keep it visually fragile but magical.

Omit:

- Legs, facial details, staff, and robe folds.
