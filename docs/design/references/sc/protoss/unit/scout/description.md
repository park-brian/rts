# Scout

Reference image: `liquipedia-scout.jpg`

The reference is a fighter with a central body and strong wing arms. Use a starfighter silhouette.

Distinctive features:

- Central pointed fuselage.
- Two wing arms spreading diagonally.
- Blue/energy tips.
- Symmetric aircraft posture.

SVG plan:

- `panels`: center diamond/wedge plus two wing polygons.
- `lines`: wing-tip strokes, center spine.
- `cores`: cockpit/core near center.
- Make it more angular than Shuttle, larger than Interceptor.

Omit:

- Tiny weapon mounts, underside detail, and complex wing plating.
