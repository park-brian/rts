# Lurker

Reference image: `liquipedia-lurker.jpg`

The reference is a low spined creature. Since its gameplay identity is burrowed line splash, emphasize a flat body with long lateral spikes.

Distinctive features:

- Low elongated central body.
- Long side spikes/spines.
- Small head/front.
- Burrowed/ground-hugging posture.

SVG plan:

- `panels`: flattened oval/body plate.
- `lines`: long side spike strokes, center ridge.
- `cores`: small head/core.
- Make it much wider and lower than Hydralisk.

Omit:

- Full legs, detailed tail, and many small spines.
