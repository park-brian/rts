# Hydralisk

Reference image: `liquipedia-hydralisk.jpg`

The reference is a cobra-like ranged unit, but the pure top-down glyph should be roughly Marine-like translated into Zerg anatomy: mandibles/head at the front, a longer torso, and curved spine pauldrons raking forward. The mandibles are important because they are the Zerg tell.

Distinctive features:

- Mandibles at the front.
- Longer narrow torso behind the head.
- Curved spine pauldrons on the left and right, raking forward.
- Slight tail/base taper.

SVG plan:

- `panels`: mandible/head wedge and longer tapered torso.
- `lines`: two forward-raking curved spine arcs; optional single rib line.
- `cores`: head or chest core.
- Make it read like the Marine rig's shoulder silhouette, but organic and predatory.

Omit:

- Teeth rows, many tiny spines, legs/feet, underside detail, and detailed underbody.
