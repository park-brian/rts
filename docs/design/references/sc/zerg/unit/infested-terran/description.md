Reference image: `liquipedia-infested-terran.jpg`

The reference is a humanoid infected unit with a swollen explosive body. It should read as unstable, small, and bulbous.

- Swollen central explosive sac.
- Tiny head.
- Uneven organic outline.

- `panels`: bulbous torso and small head.
- `lines`: two short limb strokes, sac seam.
- `cores`: large explosive belly core.
- Keep it distinct from Marine by making the torso round and organic.
