# Spider Mine

Reference image: `liquipedia-spider-mine.jpg`

The reference shows small deployed mines. The gameplay sprite should be tiny and readable as a hazard.

Distinctive features:

- Small central body.
- Three-prong or tripod shape.
- Bright warning core.
- Very low profile.

SVG plan:

- `panels`: small triangle or hex body.
- `lines`: three short legs/prongs.
- `cores`: central warning dot.
- Keep it smaller than all infantry but visible.

Omit:

- Detailed legs, shadows, and mechanical underside.
