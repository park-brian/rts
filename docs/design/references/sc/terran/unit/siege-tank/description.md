# Siege Tank

Reference image: `liquipedia-siege-tank.jpg`

The reference is a chunky tracked vehicle with a central turret. Top-down identity is simple: two tread slabs, body block, forward cannon.

Distinctive features:

- Two large side tread blocks.
- Rectangular central hull.
- Central turret plate.
- One forward barrel.

SVG plan:

- `panels`: left/right treads, central rectangle, turret polygon.
- `lines`: tread segment marks, barrel line/rect, turret seam.
- `cores`: small turret core.
- Keep it heavy and rectangular.

Omit:

- Tiny armor plates, perspective height, decals, and multiple small guns.
