# Valkyrie

Reference image: `liquipedia-valkyrie.jpg`

The Valkyrie should keep some boxy transport ancestry, but it is not a copy of the Dropship. Its front bevels run much farther down the body, the side rectangles collapse into small winglets at the lower outer edges, and it uses two centered rear thrusters.

Distinctive features:

- Slimmer box body than the Dropship.
- Top/front bevels that end lower on the body than the Dropship bevels.
- Two larger winglets at the lower outer wing ends, bottom-aligned to the body.
- Two centered rear thrusters, squat and wide, close together.

SVG plan:

- `panels`: slimmer body polygon with long bevels, two larger bottom-aligned winglet polygons, two squat wide centered rear thruster rectangles.
- `lines`: none by default.
- `cores`: optional cockpit/core near the front.
- Keep the silhouette thinner than the Dropship while still heavier than Wraith.

Omit:

- Individual missile pods/holes and long elegant wings.
