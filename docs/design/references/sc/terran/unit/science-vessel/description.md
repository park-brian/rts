# Science Vessel

Reference image: `liquipedia-science-vessel.jpg`

The reference is a round hovering detector/caster, but the top-down glyph should be a clean detector symbol: a circle with three wedge blades, almost like a nuclear radiation sign. The three small dots sit between the wedge blades, not on top of them, and the outer circle follows the outer edge of the wedge/dot layout.

Distinctive features:

- Large outer circle drawn at the outer edge of the symbol.
- Three evenly spaced wedge blades/radiation-sign panels.
- Three small circles placed between the wedge blades.
- Hovering caster identity, no weapon barrel.

SVG plan:

- `panels`: outer circle, three wedge panels, three small circular pods between the wedges.
- `lines`: circular ring and three short radial separators if needed.
- `cores`: center sensor glow.
- Keep it almost emblem-like so it reads instantly as detector/caster.

Omit:

- Tall tower perspective, tiny legs, small paneling, detached antenna clutter, and weapon-like barrels.
