# Medic

Reference image: `liquipedia-medic.jpg`

The reference is a white/red infantry unit with medical gear, but the pure top-down glyph should be a Marine variant: head circle, small side circles, visor, and a cross on the back half of the helmet. It should read as support, not weapon.

Distinctive features:

- Marine-like head circle.
- Smaller side circles than Marine.
- Simple cross as the key medical identifier, placed on the back half of the helmet circle.
- No rifle silhouette.

SVG plan:

- `panels`: two small side circles, head circle.
- `lines`: Marine-style visor plus simple cross using two short strokes on the back half of the helmet.
- `cores`: bright central heal core.
- Use a calmer, more circular outline than Marine/Firebat.

Omit:

- Staff, weapon, floating cross, face, individual arms, legs, boots, backpack clutter, underside detail, and complex medical equipment.
