# Ghost

Reference image: `liquipedia-ghost.jpg`

The reference is a slim infantry/sniper silhouette, but the pure top-down glyph should be a Marine variant: small head circle, small side circles, and a long gun rectangle. Think "Marine sniper" before "cloaked person."

Distinctive features:

- Small Marine-like head circle.
- Smaller side circles than Marine.
- Long rifle/gun rectangle, clearly longer than Marine's, drawn below the small side circles in visual z-order.
- Very small connector body.

SVG plan:

- `panels`: long rifle rectangle first, then two small side circles, then small head circle.
- `lines`: one extra-long sniper rifle bar attached through the hands; one visor line.
- `cores`: tiny eye/visor dot.
- Keep the outline narrow and stealthy.

Omit:

- Full body, legs, boots, face, hair, goggles detail, small pouches, floating rifle, underside detail, and complex cloak folds.
