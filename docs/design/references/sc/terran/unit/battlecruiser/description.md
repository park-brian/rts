# Battlecruiser

Reference image: `liquipedia-battlecruiser.jpg`

The reference is a large capital ship, but the SVG target should be an H-shaped or hammerhead-shaped top-down silhouette. It should feel huge through simple mass: smaller front trapezoid wing pair, narrow middle/neck, and larger rear trapezoid wing pair.

Distinctive features:

- Four sideways trapezoid wings: smaller front pair, larger rear pair.
- Narrow middle body/neck.
- Two very long, thick lasers placed at the outer rear wingtip edges.
- Square Yamato cannon block at the front of the spine.
- Front bridge extends down to the bottom edge of the front trapezoid wings.
- Two small rear thruster cores emit engine light backward.
- Heavy capital-ship footprint.

SVG plan:

- `panels`: four mirrored sideways trapezoid wing polygons, narrow middle rectangle, square Yamato cannon block with a short bridge aligned to the front-wing bottom edge, two very long laser rectangles at the rear wingtip edges.
- `cores`: two small rear thruster cores.
- `lines`: one center spine plus a clear Yamato cannon suggestion.
- `cores`: large bridge/core glow.
- Use more mass than every other Terran air unit, but keep the H/hammerhead silhouette primary.

Omit:

- Small turrets, windows, tiny deck markings, generic long-ship shape, swept-back Wraith wings, and perspective height.
