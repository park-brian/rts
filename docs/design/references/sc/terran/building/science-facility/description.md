# Science Facility

Top-down primitive target: a large research dome connected to several smaller satellite pods and a compact connector block. It should be rounder and more advanced than the Engineering Bay.

SVG parts in the current draft:

- `connector-block`: rectangular lab connector.
- `main-dome`: large research dome.
- `pod-a`, `pod-b`, `pod-c`: smaller satellite lab pods.
- `lab-core`: central research glow.

Animation/implementation notes: pulse `main-dome` and `lab-core` for research. Pods can pulse independently to show add-on or tech activity later.

Do not draw: vertical observatory towers, window rows, wall panels, or tiny technical machinery.
