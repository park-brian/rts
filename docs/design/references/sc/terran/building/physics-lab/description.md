# Physics Lab

Top-down primitive target: an add-on pad with a central pivot and one long instrument capsule pointing forward. It should read as a specialized research attachment.

SVG parts in the current draft:

- `addon-pad`: compact add-on base.
- `pivot`: circular instrument base.
- `instrument-capsule`: long rounded instrument body.
- `instrument-nose`: small forward core at the tip.

Animation/implementation notes: pulse `instrument-capsule` or `instrument-nose` during advanced research. If later animated, the capsule can subtly aim or charge from the pivot.

Do not draw: tall telescopes, scaffolding, angled supports, or complex lab machinery.
