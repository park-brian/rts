# Engineering Bay

Top-down primitive target: a rounded lab block with one side dome and three landing/foot disks. The identity feature is the offset dome, not fine technical detail.

SVG parts in the current draft:

- `lab-body`: rounded rectangular research body.
- `side-dome`: large side circular module for upgrade activity.
- `front-foot`, `lower-left-foot`, `lower-right-foot`: three small circular support/landing points.
- `connector-a`: straight internal connector line.

Animation/implementation notes: pulse `side-dome` during upgrades. The three feet are static alignment points; keep them simple and evenly placed so the building does not become visually noisy.

Do not draw: tool racks, antenna clutter, side walls, exposed engines, or perspective roof plating.
