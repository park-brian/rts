# Armory

Top-down primitive target: a central forge ring with four perimeter posts, cross connector arms, and a small core. It should feel like an upgrade hub and remain radially clean.

SVG parts in the current draft:

- `forge-ring`: central circular forge.
- `north-post`, `south-post`, `west-post`, `east-post`: four evenly spaced perimeter posts.
- `north-south-arm`, `west-east-arm`: cross connectors.
- `forge-core`: center upgrade light.

Animation/implementation notes: pulse `forge-core` and `forge-ring` during upgrades. The four posts should stay perfectly balanced around the center.

Do not draw: weapon racks, armor shelves, wall height, complex machinery, or isometric roof panels.
