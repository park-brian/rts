# Factory

Top-down primitive target: a large industrial rectangle with two vehicle bay slots, three round vents, and a rear stack block. It should be heavier and more mechanical than the barracks.

SVG parts in the current draft:

- `body`: main factory hull.
- `left-vehicle-bay`, `right-vehicle-bay`: two dark production exits.
- `vent-a`, `vent-b`, `vent-c`: three circular machinery vents.
- `rear-stack`: compact rear machinery block.

Animation/implementation notes: vehicle bay slots can open or glow during production. The three vents can pulse in sequence to show factory activity. Keep the bay spacing symmetric.

Do not draw: conveyor belts, wall height, cranes, detailed roof machinery, or angled shadows.
